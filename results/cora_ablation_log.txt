======================================================================
                           ANALYSIS
======================================================================

✓ GCN outperforms MLP by 26.40% on test accuracy!

This demonstrates the power of graph convolution:
  • GCN leverages neighbor information through adjacency matrix
  • Each node's feature is a weighted average of its neighbors
  • This 'homophily' assumption works well on citation networks:
    'Papers citing similar papers have similar topics'

Training vs Test Gap Analysis:
  • Train gap: -1.43% (GCN worse on training)
  • Test gap:  +26.40% (GCN better on testing)
  • The test improvement is larger than train improvement,
    suggesting graph structure provides better generalization!
